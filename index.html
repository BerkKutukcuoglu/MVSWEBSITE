about us

Who are we and what do we do?

We are Bau Metaverse Society. Our members are student of Bahcesehir University. All of our member are curious about Metaverse and they have studied about Metaverse for a long time.

As Bau Metaverse we organize a lot of events that lead people to notice and to know metaverse. Moreover, we have built some metaverse projects and we help people who try to build metaverse projects.

Finally, as Bau Metaverse Soceity, we want to be part of Metaverse as a user and builder.
